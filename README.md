This Privacy Policy describes how **EverMark** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, processes, and discloses information in connection with our WhatsApp chatbot service ("Service"), which is built using the Meta/WhatsApp Business Platform to provide automated customer support.

By messaging our WhatsApp number or otherwise interacting with our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

\---

## 1\. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information collected automatically, and information we receive through the WhatsApp Business Platform (operated by Meta).

### a. Information You Provide

* **Contact information:** your WhatsApp phone number, name, and profile information made available to us via WhatsApp.
* **Message content:** the text, images, documents, voice notes, location data, or other content you send us during a conversation.
* **Support details:** any information you share to help us resolve your inquiry (e.g., order numbers, account details, complaint descriptions).

### b. Information Collected Automatically

* **Technical/usage data:** message timestamps, delivery and read status, conversation metadata, and device/platform information made available through the WhatsApp Business API.
* **Interaction data:** how you interact with our chatbot, including button selections, menu choices, and conversation flow data, used to improve the support experience.

### c. Information We Receive from Meta/WhatsApp

As a business using the WhatsApp Business Platform, we receive limited account and messaging information from Meta in accordance with Meta's [WhatsApp Business Data Processing Terms](https://www.facebook.com/legal/whatsapp/business-data-processing-terms) and [Platform Terms](https://developers.facebook.com/terms#privacypolicy), including your WhatsApp ID, phone number, and message delivery status.

We do **not** collect any information from your WhatsApp account beyond what is necessary to provide and improve our customer support Service, and we do not access your contacts, chats with other users, or any data outside of your direct conversation with our chatbot.

## 2\. How We Use Your Information

We process the information described above for the following purposes:

* **To provide customer support:** to respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and deliver automated and/or human-assisted support through our chatbot.
* **To operate and improve the chatbot:** to train, test, and improve the accuracy and relevance of automated responses, and to identify common issues so we can improve our Service.
* **To maintain support history:** to keep a record of past conversations so we can provide continuity of support if you message us again (where applicable — see Section 3).
* **To ensure security and prevent abuse:** to detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, spam, or misuse of our Service.
* **To comply with legal obligations:** to meet recordkeeping, regulatory, or legal requirements applicable to our business.

We only respond to conversations that you initiate with us. We do not use your WhatsApp number to send unsolicited marketing or promotional messages, except where you have given explicit opt-in consent and consistent with WhatsApp's messaging policies.
